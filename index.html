Hackers exploit system vulnerabilities to access sensitive data, threatening cybersecurity. These breaches endanger personal information and network integrity. As technology evolves, so do cyber threats. Encryption, firewalls, and regular updates help mitigate risks. Cybersecurity is the process of protecting digital information and systems from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. With the increasing use of the internet, cybercriminals are attempting to breach systems using new methods. To ensure effective cybersecurity, techniques such as encryption, firewalls, and multi-factor authentication are used. Cyberattacks like phishing, malware, and ransomware pose significant threats, so regular updates and monitoring are essential for protection. Building a secure digital environment through cybersecurity is crucial for safety.
